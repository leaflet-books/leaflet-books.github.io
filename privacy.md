# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

</div>

Leaflet is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

## The Short Version

- **We don't store your photos.** They're processed and discarded.
- **Your scan history stays on your device.** We don't have it.
- **No account required.** We don't collect personal information.
- **No selling data.** Ever.

---

## What We Collect

### Photos You Take
When you scan a bookshelf, your photo is sent to our server for text recognition. The image is **processed in memory only** and is not saved to any database or disk. Once we extract the book titles, the image is discarded.

### Scan History
Your history of scanned books is stored **locally on your device only**. We don't have access to it. Delete it anytime in the app settings.

### Anonymous Usage
We may collect anonymous, aggregated statistics (like "how many scans happened today") to improve the app. This contains no personal information.

---

## Third-Party Services

### Google Cloud Vision
We use Google's Vision API to read text from your bookshelf photos. Per [Google's data usage policy](https://cloud.google.com/vision/docs/data-usage):
- Images are processed in memory and not stored
- Google does not use your images to train their models
- No image retention after processing

### Book Data APIs
We look up book information (ratings, covers, descriptions) from services like Open Library and Google Books. We send them book titles or ISBNs — never your photos or personal information.

### Affiliate Links
When you tap to buy a book, we may use affiliate links to Bookshop.org or Amazon. These services have their own privacy policies. We always show library options first.

---

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ Create accounts or profiles
- ❌ Store your photos anywhere
- ❌ Track your location
- ❌ Sell or share your data
- ❌ Show you ads

---

## Your Rights

**Delete your data:** Your scan history is stored only on your device. Delete it anytime via app settings, or uninstall the app.

**No account to delete:** Since we don't create accounts, there's nothing to delete on our end.
