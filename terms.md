## What Leaflet Does

Leaflet is a book discovery tool. You scan a bookshelf, we identify the books, and show you ratings from other readers.

**Important to understand:**
- Ratings come from sites like Goodreads and Open Library — we don't create them
- OCR (text recognition) isn't perfect — some books won't be identified
- Availability and pricing info may be outdated
- This is a tool to help you discover books, not a replacement for your own judgment

## Affiliate Links

We may earn a small commission when you buy books through links in the app (like Bookshop.org or Amazon). This is how we keep Leaflet free.

**Our commitment:**
- We always show library/free options first
- Affiliate relationships don't affect which books we highlight
- We recommend books based on ratings, not what pays us
