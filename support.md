# Support

Have questions? We're here to help.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does Leaflet work?

Point your camera at a bookshelf and tap scan. Leaflet reads the book spines, looks up each title in our database, and shows you ratings from real readers. Green highlights mean highly-rated books worth checking out.

### Why can't Leaflet identify some books?

A few reasons this happens:
- **Worn or damaged spines** — faded text is hard to read
- **Unusual fonts** — decorative typography can confuse OCR
- **Dense shelves** — books packed tightly together may blend
- **Obscure books** — older or rare books may not be in our database

Try scanning problem sections up close, or in better lighting.

### Where do the ratings come from?

We use crowdsourced ratings from sites like Goodreads and Open Library. These are ratings from real readers — not publishers, not algorithms, not us.

### Is Leaflet free?

Yes! We make money through affiliate links when you choose to buy books, but we always show library options first. No ads, no subscriptions.

### Do you store my photos?

No. Photos are processed to extract text, then immediately discarded. Your scan history is stored only on your device — we never see it.

### How do I delete my data?

Your scan history is stored locally on your phone. Delete it anytime in the app settings, or simply uninstall the app. Since we don't create accounts, there's nothing to delete on our end.
